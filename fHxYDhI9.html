Penguins are a rare bird, with many of them being at risk from human activity. Three penguin species are endangered, nine are vulnerable, and two are near threatened. Penguins are in danger because of various reasons such as: hunting them for oil, taking their eggs, destroying their natural environment, and various other reasons. We need to do all that we cans for these little guys so that they can get off the endangered and vulnerable species lists, and grow in population size once again.
